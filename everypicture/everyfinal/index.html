damn, i wish my mom was here

with the new pictures i take,

click to flip card

i wonder if older ones will fade away

meeting new people always

leads me back to the eyes of her, the one gave me everything

one bad day can never compare to

infinite bad days, in hopes of seeing sun someday

some art pieces make me giggle

and some remind me the ones from the past

these days, i am physically closer to new friends

but i will always crave those from home

i hope my mom can see this too one day
